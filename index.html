What is Placenta Encapsulation?
Placentophagy is a natural nutritional supplement after giving birth. The purpose is to return the beneficial nutrients to the body such as vitamins, minerals, hormones, proteins and iron. The benefits of placenta consumption are supported by ongoing research but have not been evaluated by the Food and Drug Administration. Studies show restoring these important nutrients provides a quicker healing process for a more balanced body function. Anectodal evidence confirms placenta medicine prevents or decreases postpartum depression, increases breast-milk production, increases energy levels and overall well being.
